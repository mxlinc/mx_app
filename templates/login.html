At MX Learning, we are committed to delivering personalized math learning to students across North America. Every weekend, we curate customized work packages based on each student’s progress. These packages include concise video lessons introducing new concepts, interactive online quizzes and practice tests and optional worksheets for extra practice.

Our human-in-the-loop approach ensures students have direct access to the teacher preparing their work. They can review submissions with the teacher and ask questions during live office hours on Sundays 4:00 to 5:00 PM ET & Thursdays: 8:00 to 9:00 PM ET.
