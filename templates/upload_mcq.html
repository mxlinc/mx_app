Bulk-import questions from a plain text file.

MCQ format:
question=Emma has x stickers. She buys 4 more. Which expression shows the total?
opt1=$x+4$
opt2=$4x$
opt3=$x-4$
opt4=$4-x$

opt1 is always the correct answer. Blank lines between blocks are optional.
Fill format:
question=Evaluate the following:
label1=$-3+4$
answer:1
label2=$3+4$
answer:7

question=What is the value of $x$ in $2x-2=0$?
label1=$x=$
answer:1

One or more label/answer pairs per question. Missing or non-numeric answers default to 0. All blanks are numeric type.
